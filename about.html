At MAK, our purpose is delivering business value through our people, processes, and technology to our diverse industry clients. With a combined 100 years of work experience from the leadership team, we ensure the solutions we offer to our clients are tailored, bold and innovative. We support our clients’ needs from initial discovery and evaluation all the way through to program execution and multi-year production support outsourcing. Our single, cohesive model simplifies your customer experience and ensures your satisfaction. They are the foundation of our success.

Our promise is to champion diversity, build an inclusive culture, and do our part to create a more equitable environment. At MAK, you are welcome for who you are, no matter where you come from, what you look like, or how you identify yourself. Ensuring that we are always recruiting, retaining, and promoting a diverse mix of colleagues who are representative of our company gives us a great opportunity to have access to a broad range of ideas and allows us to give you wide variety of creative and innovative solutions. We believe that when people feel respected and included, they can be more creative, innovative, and successful.

MAK Technologies is deeply committed to social responsibility, with a focus on sustainable, equitable and just development. We take pride in our involvement with community projects and volunteer work, supporting global initiatives to create positive change and improve the well-being of underprivileged communities. Our dedication to social responsibility is an integral part of our corporate standards.
